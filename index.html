About Us

Maya and Aurelia’s DayCare is a safe, nurturing, and engaging environment where children aged 6–11 can learn, grow, and have fun. Run by qualified carers and educators, we focus on creativity, play-based learning, and strong values.

Programs We Offer

  • Homework Help & Tutoring
  • Creative Arts & Crafts
  • Outdoor Games & Sports
  • STEM Learning Activities
  • Quiet Time & Reading Zone

Why Ages 6–11?

We focus on children ages 6–11 because these are the years of independence, imagination, and discovery. Our staff understand the needs of primary-aged children and design activities that build confidence and lifelong skills.

Daily Activities

Our daily schedule includes time for active play, creativity, snack breaks, group learning, and free time. Children always leave with a smile!

Our Location

We are located in a quiet, safe neighborhood with easy parking and public transport access. Drop-offs and pickups are stress-free!
